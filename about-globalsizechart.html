About GlobalSizeChart

Quick answer:

GlobalSizeChart is a data-driven reference for international shoe and clothing size conversion—methodology, sources, and editorial standards.

GlobalSizeChart is a data-driven reference for international shoe and clothing size conversion. We publish converters, charts, and educational pages so people can map US, UK, EU, Japan, China, and centimeter-based measurements consistently.

What we do

  • Free size converters for shoes and clothing with transparent methodology.
  • Programmatic conversion pages for common queries (e.g. EU to US shoe size), each with quick answers and structured data.
  • Guides and knowledge hubs on how sizing differs by region and brand.
  • An index of conversion answers for discovery (search, assistants, and citation-friendly HTML).

Data methodology

Conversions are built from length-based mapping (foot length in centimeters where applicable), aligned with widely published international sizing references. Brand fit still varies—we always recommend checking the brand’s own chart for final purchases.

Common questions

How should I use this converter?

Pick your region and size, then read the equivalents. When in doubt, measure in centimeters and compare to the brand chart.

Why do sizes differ between countries?

Each region uses its own scale; mapping through foot length (cm) reduces error.
