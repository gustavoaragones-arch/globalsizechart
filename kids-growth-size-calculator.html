Growth tip: Kids' feet grow quickly. Check length every few months. Allow about 1–1.5 cm room at the toe; when there's less than half a thumb width, size up.

How to measure kids' feet

Have your child stand on a piece of paper against a wall. Mark the tip of the longest toe and measure from the wall to the mark in cm. Use the larger foot. See our Kids Printable Shoe Size Guide for a full chart.
