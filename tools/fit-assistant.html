FAQ

How does the Fit Assistant work?

The Fit Assistant uses only local, deterministic rules. It matches your foot length and measurements to standard size charts, applies your fit preference (tight/regular/loose), and adds brand-based fit notes. No data is sent to any server.

Why does it suggest alternative sizes?

If you prefer a tighter or looser fit, the tool suggests half a size up or down. You can also see alternatives for regular fit to compare.

Are recommendations accurate for every brand?

Recommendations are based on standard conversion charts. Brands vary; the fit warnings reflect common patterns (e.g. runs small). Always check the brand's own size guide for the specific product.
