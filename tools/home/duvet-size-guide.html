Duvet Size Guide (US, UK, EU)

Duvet and comforter dimensions differ by country: US sizes often use inches and names like Full/Queen/King; the UK uses single, double, king, and super king with different width and drop conventions; the EU typically labels products in centimetres. Always match the duvet to your mattress width and length (and account for mattress depth if you use a deep fitted sheet).
