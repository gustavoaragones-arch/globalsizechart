US to EU Shoe Size Conversion Chart

US and European (EU) shoe sizes use different numbering systems. EU sizes are often related to foot length in centimeters and typically run higher numbers than US for the same fit. Converting correctly helps when buying from European brands or shopping in EU markets. Our conversion uses a single centimeter (foot length) baseline: each size is mapped to a foot length in cm, and that cm value is matched to the closest size in the target system. FAQ

What is US size 9 in EU?

US men's 9 converts to EU 42. US women's 9 converts to EU 40. Use the table or linked size pages for your gender.

Are US and EU shoe sizes the same?

No. EU sizes use a different scale. US 9 men's is typically EU 42.

How do I convert US shoe sizes to EU?

Use the table above or the Shoe Size Converter. Conversions use a common centimeter (foot length) baseline.
