UK and US shoe sizes use different scales: a UK 8 is not the same as a US 8. US sizes run about one size larger than UK for the same foot length, so converting correctly matters when buying from American brands or shopping in the US. Our conversion uses a single centimeter (foot length) baseline: each size is mapped to a foot length in cm, and that cm value is matched to the closest size in the target system. FAQ

What is UK size 8 in US?

UK men's 8 converts to US 9. UK women's 8 converts to US 10. Use the table or linked size pages for your gender.

Are UK and US shoe sizes the same?

No. US sizes run about one size larger than UK for the same foot length.

How do I convert UK shoe sizes to US?

Use the table above or the Shoe Size Converter. Conversions use a common centimeter (foot length) baseline.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EU 42 in US shoe size?

EU 42 typically converts to US men's size 9 and US women's size 10.5, depending on brand and width. Use any converter on this page when available.

Are EU and US shoe sizes the same?

No. The EU sizing system and US shoe sizing scale use different scales. International shoe size conversion is most reliable when you anchor to foot length in cm.
