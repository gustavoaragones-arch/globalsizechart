Quick answer:

EU to UK shoe size conversion chart. Convert European sizes to UK with table and links to each size pair.

European (EU) and UK shoe sizes use different scales. EU sizes are related to foot length in centimeters; UK sizes use a separate scale that runs roughly one size smaller than US for the same foot length. Converting correctly helps when buying from British brands or comparing EU and UK size charts. Our conversion uses a single centimeter (foot length) baseline: each size is mapped to a foot length in cm, and that cm value is matched to the closest size in the target system. FAQ

What is EU size 42 in UK?

EU 42 converts to UK 8 (men's). Use the table or linked size pages for your gender.

Are EU and UK shoe sizes the same?

No. EU and UK use different scales. EU 42 is UK 8 in men's.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EU 42 in US shoe size?

EU 42 typically converts to US men's size 9 and US women's size 10.5, depending on brand and width. Use any converter on this page when available.

Are EU and US shoe sizes the same?

No. The EU sizing system and US shoe sizing scale use different scales. International shoe size conversion is most reliable when you anchor to foot length in cm.
