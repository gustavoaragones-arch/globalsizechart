US to UK Shoe Size Conversion Chart

US and UK shoe sizes use different scales: a US 9 is not the same as a UK 9. US sizes run about one size larger than UK for the same foot length, so converting correctly matters when buying from British brands or shopping in the UK. Our conversion logic uses a single centimeter (foot length) baseline: each size is mapped to a foot length in cm, and that cm value is then matched to the closest size in the target system. That way EU half-sizes, UK whole and half sizes, and US steps are all consistent. The table below is generated from the same dataset that powers the calculator. Each row links to a dedicated conversion page with converter and equivalents.

FAQ

What is US size 9 in UK?

US men's 9 converts to UK 8. US women's 9 converts to UK 7. Use the table or the linked size pages for your gender.

Are UK and US shoe sizes the same?

No. UK sizes run about one size smaller than US for the same foot length.

How do I convert US shoe sizes to UK?

Use the table above or the Shoe Size Converter. Conversions use a common centimeter (foot length) baseline.
