About GlobalSizeChart.com

Who We Are

GlobalSizeChart.com is an independent website dedicated to providing free, accurate size conversion tools for international shoppers. We are not affiliated with any specific retailer or brand. Our goal is to make international shopping easier by helping you understand and convert between different sizing systems used around the world.

Our Mission

GlobalSizeChart.com is a free, easy-to-use tool designed to help you convert sizes between different international sizing systems. Whether you're shopping online from international retailers, traveling, or simply need to understand size differences between regions, we're here to help.

What We Offer

Our platform provides accurate size conversion tools for:

  • Shoe Sizes: Convert between US, UK, EU, Japan, China, and CM measurements
  • Clothing Sizes: Convert tops, pants, and dresses across all major sizing systems
  • Comprehensive Guides: Learn how to measure yourself accurately and understand regional sizing differences

Why Use Our Converter?

  • Free: All our tools are completely free to use
  • Accurate: Based on standard international sizing conventions
  • Easy to Use: Simple, intuitive interface that works on all devices
  • Comprehensive: Covers all major sizing systems and regions
  • No Registration: Use our tools without creating an account

Important Note

While we strive to provide accurate conversions, it's important to remember that:

  • Size conversions are approximate and based on standard conventions
  • Sizing can vary significantly between brands and manufacturers
  • Always check brand-specific size charts when available
  • Consider your personal fit preferences (slim, regular, oversized)

Our tool is designed to provide a helpful starting point, but individual fit preferences and brand variations should always be considered.
