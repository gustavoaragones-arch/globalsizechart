About GlobalSizeChart.com

Publisher Identity

GlobalSizeChart.com is operated by the publisher of this website. We are an independent publisher focused on size conversion and measurement guides. We are not owned by, affiliated with, or funded by any clothing or shoe brand, retailer, or manufacturer.

Contact: contact@globalsizechart.com. For more ways to reach us, see our contact page.

Purpose of This Site

Our purpose is to provide free, practical size conversion tools and educational content so you can convert between international sizing systems (e.g., US, UK, EU, Japan, CM) for shoes and clothing. We aim to help you get a reliable starting point for size selection when shopping across regions. We do not sell products; we only provide conversion tools and guides.

Who We Are

GlobalSizeChart.com is an independent website dedicated to providing free size conversion tools for international shoppers. We are not affiliated with any specific retailer or brand. Our goal is to make international shopping easier by helping you understand and convert between different sizing systems used around the world.

Our Mission

We provide a free, easy-to-use tool to convert sizes between different international sizing systems. Whether you're shopping online from international retailers, traveling, or need to understand size differences between regions, we aim to help with accurate conversions and clear guidance.

What We Offer

Our platform provides accurate size conversion tools for:

  • Shoe Sizes: Convert between US, UK, EU, Japan, China, and CM measurements
  • Clothing Sizes: Convert tops, pants, and dresses across all major sizing systems
  • Comprehensive Guides: Learn how to measure yourself accurately and understand regional sizing differences

Important Note

While we strive to provide accurate conversions, it's important to remember that:

  • Size conversions are approximate and based on standard conventions
  • Sizing can vary significantly between brands and manufacturers
  • Always check brand-specific size charts when available
  • Consider your personal fit preferences (slim, regular, oversized)

Our tool is designed to provide a helpful starting point, but individual fit preferences and brand variations should always be considered.
