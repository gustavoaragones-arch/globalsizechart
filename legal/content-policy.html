Content Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

What This Policy Covers

This page describes what kind of content we publish on GlobalSizeChart.com and the standards we aim to follow. We do not allow user-generated content (reviews, comments, or posts) on the main site; all published content is created by the publisher.

Purpose of Our Content

Our content exists to:

  • Provide free size conversion tools (shoe and clothing) between major international sizing systems.
  • Explain how sizing works by region and category (e.g., US, UK, EU, Japan, CM).
  • Offer practical guidance on measuring and using conversion results.
  • Link to related tools and guides within our site to help you find the right converter or guide.

Content Standards

  • Accuracy: We base conversions on standard international sizing conventions. We do not guarantee a perfect fit for every brand or product; we aim for a reliable starting point.
  • Clarity: We write in plain language so that visitors can understand how to use our tools and how to interpret results.
  • Independence: Our content is not written or influenced by brands or retailers in exchange for payment. See our Editorial Policy for more.
  • No medical or professional advice: Our content is for general information only. It is not medical, legal, or professional advice.

What We Do Not Publish

We do not knowingly publish:

  • False or deliberately misleading sizing information.
  • Content that promotes harm, discrimination, or illegal activity.
  • Spam, malware, or deceptive practices.
  • User-generated reviews or comments (we do not host these on this site).

Third-Party Links

We may link to external sites (e.g., brand size charts or measurement guides). We do not control those sites and are not responsible for their content or practices. Links are provided for convenience and do not imply endorsement.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this content policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised when we do. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact
