Monetization Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Why This Page Exists

We want you to know how GlobalSizeChart.com may earn revenue. This page explains current and potential future monetization so you can understand how the site is funded and how that might affect what you see.

Current Monetization

As of the date above, our primary goal is to provide free, useful size conversion tools and guides. We may in the future introduce one or more of the following to help support the site:

  • Advertising: We may display advertisements on our site (e.g., through programs such as Google AdSense or similar). Those ads would be provided by third-party ad networks. We would not control the exact ads shown to each user; ad networks use their own criteria (including cookies and browsing data) to choose ads. We would follow common practices for ad placement and labeling (e.g., "Ad" or "Sponsored" where required).
  • Affiliate links: We may add links to retailers or brands that pay us a commission if you make a purchase after clicking. Such links would be disclosed where we use them (e.g., in a disclaimer or on the page). Affiliate relationships would not determine which sizes or brands we recommend in our conversion tools or guides; our editorial standards remain independent. See our Editorial Policy.

How Monetization Affects Content

We do not sell placement in our conversion results or in our guides. Our size conversion logic and sizing advice are based on standard conventions and editorial judgment, not on which advertisers or affiliates pay us. If we add ads or affiliate links, we will keep them separate from the core conversion tools and will disclose them as described above.

No Pay-for-Placement in Our Tools

Brands and retailers cannot pay to have their sizes favored or to appear in our conversion results. Our conversion tables and recommendations are not for sale. Any future affiliate or ad partnerships would not change how we calculate or display size conversions.

User Experience

If we introduce ads, we will aim to keep them clearly identifiable and to avoid disrupting the main use of our converters and guides. We will not use pop-ups, misleading buttons, or intrusive formats that violate common ad policies (e.g., Google’s program policies) where we participate in such programs.

Changes to This Policy

If we add new forms of monetization or change how we use ads or affiliates, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

Questions about monetization can be sent to contact@globalsizechart.com or via our contact page.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EU 42 in US shoe size?

EU 42 typically converts to US men's size 9 and US women's size 10.5, depending on brand and width. Use any converter on this page when available.

Are EU and US shoe sizes the same?

No. The EU sizing system and US shoe sizing scale use different scales. International shoe size conversion is most reliable when you anchor to foot length in cm.
