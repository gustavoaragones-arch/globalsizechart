# Quora strategy — GlobalSizeChart (Phase 23)

## Format

- **Longer** than Reddit: 2–4 short paragraphs.  
- **Always** include the canonical page link at the end (Quora allows attribution when the answer is substantive).  
- Lead with the direct equivalence, then nuance (brand variance, width, cm measurement).

## Example structure

1. Direct answer (EU 42 ≈ US men’s 9 on typical charts).  
2. Caveat (brands differ; kids/women’s scales differ).  
3. **Centimeter** as the tie-breaker.  
4. Link: `https://globalsizechart.com/programmatic-pages/...`

## Starters

Use `authority/generated/quora/*.md` — customize the opening so answers aren’t duplicate content.

## Tracking

Append to `data/authority-tracker.json` → `quora_answers`.
