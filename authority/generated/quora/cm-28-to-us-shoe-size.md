**Question:**
How do I interpret CM Size 28 to US Shoe Size for shoe sizing?

**Answer (longer format; include link):**

CM size 28 converts approximately to US men's size 10. Use the converter above to see all regional equivalents and confirm your fit.

Sizing still varies by brand and width, so measuring foot length in centimeters and comparing to a length-based chart is more accurate than a single number alone.
