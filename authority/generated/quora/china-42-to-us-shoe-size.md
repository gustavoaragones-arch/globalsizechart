# china-42-to-us-shoe-size — Quora-ready

**Question:**
How do I interpret China Size 42 to US Shoe Size for shoe sizing?

**Answer (longer format; include link):**

A CHINA shoe size 42 converts to US size 9 and measures approximately 29 cm in foot length.

Sizing still varies by brand and width, so measuring foot length in centimeters and comparing to a length-based chart is more accurate than a single number alone.
