**Question:**
How do I interpret CM Size 26 to EU Shoe Size for shoe sizing?

**Answer (longer format; include link):**

CM size 26 converts approximately to EU men's size 41. Use the converter above to see all regional equivalents and confirm your fit.

Sizing still varies by brand and width, so measuring foot length in centimeters and comparing to a length-based chart is more accurate than a single number alone.
