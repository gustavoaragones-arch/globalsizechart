How do I interpret China to US Shoe Size for shoe sizing?

**Answer (longer format; include link):**

Use the converter above to convert any China shoe size to US. Results also show UK, Japan, China, and CM equivalents. Fit can vary by brand; check the brand's size chart when possible.

Sizing still varies by brand and width, so measuring foot length in centimeters and comparing to a length-based chart is more accurate than a single number alone.
