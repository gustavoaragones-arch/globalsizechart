Frequently Asked Questions

Can I save these as PDF?

Yes. Use your browser's Print option and choose "Save as PDF" to keep a copy.

How do I measure my foot for the sheet?

Follow the steps on the foot measuring sheet: paper against wall, mark longest toe, measure in cm. Use the larger foot.

Do the printable guides work on mobile?

Yes. You can view and print from a phone or tablet. For best results, use a ruler for measurements.
