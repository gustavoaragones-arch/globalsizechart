Kids Printable Shoe Size Guide

Use this guide to find your child’s shoe size in US, UK, EU, and foot length in cm. Measure at home, then check the chart below.

How to measure kids’ feet

  1. Place a piece of paper on a hard floor with one end against a wall.
  2. Have your child stand on the paper with their heel touching the wall.
  3. Mark the paper at the tip of the longest toe (use a pencil held straight).
  4. Measure from the wall to the mark in centimeters. Use the larger foot.
  5. Find that length (or the nearest) in the chart below to get US, UK, and EU sizes.
