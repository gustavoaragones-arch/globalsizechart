Foot Measuring Sheet

Use this sheet to measure your foot length in centimeters, then convert to your shoe size online.

Measurement instructions

  1. Place a piece of paper against a wall on a hard floor.
  2. Stand with your heel touching the wall. Mark the tip of your longest toe on the paper.
  3. Measure from the wall to the mark in centimeters. Repeat for both feet and use the larger measurement.
  4. Enter your measurement (in cm) into our shoe size converter to get US, UK, EU, and other sizes.
