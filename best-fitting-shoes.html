Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my best shoe size?

Measure your foot length in cm, use our shoe size converter for your region, and check the brand's size chart. Many brands run small—consider sizing up when unsure.

Which brands run small in shoes?

Nike and Adidas often run small. See our brand size guides and the brands that run small hub for fit tendencies.

Can I convert EU shoe size to US?

Yes. Use our Shoe Size Converter or the EU to US conversion pages. Enter your EU size and gender to get US, UK, and other equivalents.
