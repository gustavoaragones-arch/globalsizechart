International Shoe Size Conversion Chart

Shoe sizes vary across countries. A US size 9 is not the same as a European 42 or a UK 8. This international shoe size conversion chart helps you convert between US, UK, EU, Japan, China, and centimeter (CM) measurements. US and UK use different numeric scales; EU sizes are based on foot length in cm; Japanese sizes often follow cm. Converting correctly reduces returns and improves fit when you buy from international brands or travel. How to Measure Your Foot in CM

Measuring your foot in centimeters gives you a universal size that works with any regional chart. Stand on a sheet of paper with your heel against a wall. Mark the tip of your longest toe, then measure from the wall to the mark in cm. Repeat for both feet and use the larger measurement. Use the converter above with “Centimeters (CM)” as the source region to get your size in US, UK, EU, or JP. For a printable guide and more tips, see Measurement Tools and How to Measure Your Feet in CM.
