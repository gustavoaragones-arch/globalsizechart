How to Measure Your Foot in Centimeters

Measuring your foot in centimeters is the most accurate way to determine your shoe size. Follow these steps:

  1. Prepare: Place a piece of paper on a hard, flat surface against a wall
  2. Position: Stand on the paper with your heel against the wall
  3. Mark: Mark the longest point of your foot (usually the big toe) on the paper
  4. Measure: Measure from the wall to the mark in centimeters
  5. Repeat: Measure both feet and use the larger measurement

Pro Tips:

  • Measure your feet in the evening when they're at their largest
  • Wear the type of socks you'll wear with the shoes
  • Stand while measuring (don't sit) for accurate results
  • If you're between sizes, round up to the next half or whole size

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EU 42 in US shoe size?

EU 42 typically converts to US men's size 9 and US women's size 10.5, depending on brand and width. Use any converter on this page when available.

Are EU and US shoe sizes the same?

No. The EU sizing system and US shoe sizing scale use different scales. International shoe size conversion is most reliable when you anchor to foot length in cm.
