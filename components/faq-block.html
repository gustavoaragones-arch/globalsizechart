Frequently Asked Questions

What is EU 42 in US shoe size?

EU 42 typically converts to US men's size 9 and US women's size 10.5, depending on brand and last shape. Use the on-page converter for gender-specific equivalents.

Are EU and US shoe sizes the same?

No. The EU sizing system and US shoe sizing scale use different numbering. International shoe size conversion should use foot length in cm as the anchor when possible.
