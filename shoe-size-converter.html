How to Measure Your Shoe Size

Measuring Your Foot in Centimeters

▼

For the most accurate shoe size conversion, measure your foot in centimeters:

  1. Place a piece of paper on a hard, flat surface against a wall
  2. Stand on the paper with your heel against the wall
  3. Mark the longest point of your foot (usually the big toe)
  4. Measure from the wall to the mark in centimeters
  5. Repeat for both feet and use the larger measurement

Pro Tip: Measure your feet in the evening when they're at their largest, and wear the type of socks you'll wear with the shoes.

Understanding Shoe Size Systems

▼

US Sizing: Uses whole and half sizes. Men's and women's sizes are different scales.

UK Sizing: Typically 1 size smaller than US. Uses whole and half sizes.

EU Sizing: Uses metric-based sizing, typically 1-1.5 sizes larger than US. Uses whole sizes primarily.

Japan Sizing: Based on centimeter measurements. Generally runs smaller than US/EU.

China Sizing: Similar to Japanese sizing, metric-based.

CM (Centimeters): Direct foot length measurement - the most accurate method.

Common Shoe Size Conversions

Here are some common shoe size conversions to help you get started:

  • US Men's 9 = UK 8 = EU 42 = JP 28 = CN 42 = 27.0 cm
  • US Women's 7 = UK 5 = EU 38 = JP 25 = CN 38 = 24.0 cm
  • EU 42 = US Men's 9 = UK 8 = JP 28 = CN 42
  • UK 8 = US Men's 9 = EU 42 = JP 28 = CN 42
