Frequently Asked Questions

Are these printables free?

Yes. All our printable size charts, kids guide, and cm ruler are free. Print or save as PDF.

How do I get accurate measurements with the cm ruler?

Print the cm ruler at 100% scale (no scaling). In your print dialog, choose "Actual size" or "100%" so the ruler is true to scale.

Can I use the kids size guide for toddlers?

Yes. The kids printable guide includes toddler and children's shoe sizes in US, UK, EU, and cm.
