Brand sizing philosophy

Levi's uses US waist/inseam for jeans. Numbered waist (e.g. 32) and length (e.g. 30L) are standard.

Known inconsistencies

Fit (501, 511, etc.) changes how sizing feels. EU and UK use cm or local equivalents.

Fit tendencies
