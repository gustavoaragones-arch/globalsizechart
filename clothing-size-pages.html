Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert US clothing size to EU?

Use our clothing size converter or browse the specific conversion pages linked above. EU sizes often use different letter or number scales than US.

Are clothing sizes the same for men and women?

No. Men's and women's clothing use different size scales. Use the gender-specific pages or the main clothing converter.

What measurements do I need for clothing size?

Chest, waist, and hips in centimeters are the main measurements. See our printable measurement chart and measurement assistant tool.
