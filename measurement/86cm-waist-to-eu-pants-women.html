How to measure

For accurate conversion, measure as follows:

  1. Measure around your natural waist (above the navel, below the ribs).
  2. Keep the tape snug but not compressing the skin.
  3. Record the measurement in centimeters.

Sizing Insights

Common fit issues—too tight, too long, narrow toe box, or inconsistent between styles—often come from mixing regional sizes or not matching your measurements to the brand's chart. Measuring yourself (foot length for shoes; chest, waist, hips for clothing) and comparing to the product's size guide helps. When between sizes, sizing up usually improves comfort.

Fit Considerations

Sizing is not standardized globally. Different countries and brands use different base measurements, grading rules, and fit models. EU and Asian sizing often run smaller than US; UK shoe sizes are typically one size down from US. Within a region, brands can still differ. Use conversions as a starting point and always confirm with the retailer's size chart.

Sizing Examples

US sizing tends to be more relaxed; EU and UK often use slimmer cuts. Asian sizing (e.g. Japan, China) frequently runs smaller than US and EU. Shoe size numbers don't align across regions—a US 9 is not the same as a UK 9 or EU 42. Use a conversion chart or converter for your target region and, when possible, the brand's regional size guide.

Why sizes don’t line up everywhere

Different scales

US, UK, EU, and JP labels rarely describe the same foot length in millimeters.

Brand lasts

Two brands can label the same length differently based on shape and materials.

Use centimeters

Measuring both feet in cm is the most reliable way to cross-check any chart.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EU 42 in US shoe size?

EU 42 typically converts to US men's size 9 and US women's size 10.5, depending on brand and width. Use any converter on this page when available.

Are EU and US shoe sizes the same?

No. The EU sizing system and US shoe sizing scale use different scales. International shoe size conversion is most reliable when you anchor to foot length in cm.
